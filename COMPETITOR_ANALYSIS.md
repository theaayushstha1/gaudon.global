## Individual Competitor Deep Dives

### 1. DOW (DOWSIL)

**Website:** https://www.dow.com/en-us/product-technology/pt-adhesives-sealants.html

**Key Strengths:**
- Market-centric navigation (organized by use case, not product)
- Sustainability Product Selector Tool
- Strong brand authority with trademarked product families
- Technical precision in content (mentions mechanical stress, substrate compatibility)
- References founding date (1897) and institutional credibility

**Content Strategy:**
- Educational resources and selector guides
- Content organized by application rather than product
- Sustainability integration throughout

**Trust Signals:**
- Stock ticker (DOW) displayed
- Olympic sponsorship references
- UN Global Compact, Ellen MacArthur Foundation associations
- "Speak to an expert" CTAs

**Weaknesses:**
- No dynamic calculators
- Limited live support options
- Complex navigation for non-technical users

**What JYB Can Learn:**
- Organize content by application/use case
- Add sustainability messaging
- Display company history prominently

### 2. HENKEL (LOCTITE, BONDERITE)

**Website:** https://next.henkel-adhesives.com/us/en/services.html

**Key Strengths:**
- Sophisticated search with recent search history
- Extensive resource library (case studies, white papers, webinars, eBooks)
- E-learning platform (LOCTITE XPLORE)
- In-person training offerings
- Seven distinct contact types

**Content Strategy:**
- Service-first narrative
- Multi-format content delivery
- Partnership positioning ("we support you no matter the challenge")

**Trust Signals:**
- ROHS certificates and RDS documents
- Distributor locator tool
- Multiple support channels

**Weaknesses:**
- Lacks interactive calculators
- No product comparison tools
- Complex for first-time visitors

**What JYB Can Learn:**
- Create educational content hub (webinars, eBooks)
- Offer multiple contact channel options
- Add downloadable resources library

### 3. 3M

**Website:** https://www.3m.com/3M/en_US/bonding-and-assembly-us/

**Key Strengths (BEST-IN-CLASS TOOLS):**
- **Adhesive Sealants Product Selector** - Multi-criteria filtering
- **Bonding Product Selector** - Assembly-based questionnaire
- **Structural Adhesives Product Selector** - Dynamic filtering (substrates, temp, cure time)
- **Sprays and Non-structural Adhesives Selector** - Factor-based recommendations

**Content Strategy:**
- Industry-standard positioning
- Application-focused technical content
- Expert assistance readily available (1-800 number)

**Trust Signals:**
- "Help define the industry standard for quality"
- Technical specialist collaboration offers

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited case studies
- No live chat visible
- Brand-heavy navigation

**What JYB Can Learn:**
- **PRIORITY: Build interactive product selector tools like 3M**
- Add substrate compatibility matrix
- Create multi-criteria filtering system

## Recommended Priority Actions for JYB

### Tier 1 - High Impact / Quick Wins

1. **Add Coverage Calculator** (No competitor has this)
   - Input: Joint dimensions
   - Output: Tubes needed + estimated cost

2. **Create Warranty Display System** (Follow GE model)
   - Tiered badges on product cards
   - Dedicated warranty page

3. **Enhance Product Finder** (Follow 3M model)
   - Add substrate selection
   - Add temperature requirements
   - Add cure time preferences

4. **Add Case Studies/Project Gallery** (Follow Sika model)
   - Real projects with photos
   - Products used, challenges solved

### Tier 2 - Medium Impact / Strategic

5. **Build How-To Content Hub** (Follow DAP model)
   - Video tutorials
   - Step-by-step guides
   - Downloadable PDFs

6. **Implement Schema.org** (Follow Sika)
   - Product schema
   - FAQ schema
   - BreadcrumbList schema

7. **Create Where-to-Buy Feature** (Follow GE)
   - Distributor locator
   - Online retailer links

8. **Add Pro vs. Consumer Segmentation** (Follow DAP)
   - Different landing experiences
   - Tailored product recommendations

### Tier 3 - Lower Impact / Long-term

9. **Develop E-learning Resources** (Follow Henkel)
10. **Add Color Match Tool** (Follow DAP)
11. **Create Newsletter Signup** (Follow Gorilla)
12. **Add More Languages** (American Sealants has 9)
